About Us

Introduction

Southern Africa Dawah Network (SADN) is a South African based organization established in 1994 to assist the underprivileged communities in South Africa. Seeing the need to assist those less fortunate, the SADN focused on education, dawah and community development.

Mission

Southern Africa Dawah Network Southern Africa Dawah Network is a registered non-profit organization established about 25 years ago to serve the underprivileged communities. We are committed to the establishment and promotion of quantitative education and learning institutes that serve to promote and develop a productive generation. Our main aim is education, dawah and community development.
